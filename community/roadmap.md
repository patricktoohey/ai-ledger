# PythonMuse Roadmap

Where we're headed, and where you can help.

---

## Near Term

- Publish strong accounting automation examples (bank reconciliation, AR aging)
- Build sample datasets that are realistic and safe to share
- Create beginner-friendly setup guides for Python + AI tools
- Open the repo for contributions with clear guidance

## Mid Term

- Build reusable finance notebooks and scripts
- Add audit-ready AI templates and checklists
- Launch contributor spotlights to highlight community work
- Expand examples to cover more accounting workflows

## Long Term

- Become an open practical library for AI + Python in accounting
- Cover the full range of finance workflows: close, audit, reporting, analysis
- Build a community of accounting and finance professionals who share and learn together

---

Want to help with any of these? See [Ideas Wanted](ideas-wanted.md) or check [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md).
